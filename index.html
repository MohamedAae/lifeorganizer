Prioritizing Your Privacy

Absolutely nothing is stored online.

We do not and will not offer server space. It does not matter if you triple lock your server and hire twenty ninjas to protect it, any server is vulnerable to hacks, and your documents can be all over the web.
The only safe custodian of your documents is YOU. Everything you store on Life Organizer stays on your phone.
